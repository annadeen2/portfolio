Freelance journalist based in NYC

Anna Deen is a freelance journalist and a graduate student at CUNY's Newmark Journalism School, where she aims to specialize in investigative and data reporting. After receiving her B.A. in American Culture Studies and English Literature from Washington University in St. Louis, she moved to New York City to work as an assistant editor at Oxford University Press.

Anna has past experience writing content for several organizations, including the US Department of State and Prime Publishing. She has also moonlighted as a Radio Show Host & DJ, and worked in several modern art museums, including the Frick Collection of New York.

Right now, she's probably eating Ben & Jerry's and plotting her next trip to the farmer's market.
